# Launch, Operate, Learn, And Retire

Use this reference when the requested scope includes production launch,
migration, service operation, customer adoption, renewal, or retirement.

## Launch Baseline

Confirm scope/version, environment, data migration, security/privacy, backup,
rollback, observability, support/on-call, training, customer notice, acceptance
owner, and go/no-go decision. High-risk modules require accountable sign-off.

## Operational Contract

Record:

- service levels, business hours, escalation, incident owner;
- logs, metrics, traces, audit, data freshness, and alert thresholds;
- failed-job, dead-letter, reconciliation, manual recovery, and replay paths;
- model/prompt/tool/knowledge versions and rollback for AI behavior;
- backup, restore, disaster recovery, retention, deletion, and export;
- support knowledge, runbooks, known limitations, and customer responsibilities.

## Adoption And Customer Success

Separate system health from customer outcome. Track activation, task completion,
workflow closure, adoption by role/organization, support burden, value metric,
contract obligation, renewal risk, and expansion evidence.

## Learn

Link production learning to `METRIC`, `EVD`, `DEC`, and `CHG` IDs. Do not dump
unowned observations into a backlog. Each action has owner, hypothesis,
priority, evidence, and next review.

## Retire

Define customer notice, replacement, export/return, retention/deletion,
integration shutdown, access revocation, archive, financial/contract closure,
support end date, and orphaned workflow/data checks. Retirement is complete only
when evidence shows no active dependency or unowned regulated record remains.
